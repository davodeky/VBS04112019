Sunday's Sermon

Bible Reading

Theme: I have got to get myself together
  • Romans 12:1-2

Sunday's Sermon
  • Paul is exhorting us to get ourselves together.
  • We are being reminded that in order to get to the next level, we have got to get our act together before God.
  • The common denominator of any relationship is self.
  • God fills the spiritual need for a relationship.
  • We all have a dimension that longs for a relationship.
  • We need to get out of the shells we are in and become the social impacting being that we can be.
  • We need to get our act together in order to move together and up with God.
  • We have to present ourselves as a living sacrifice that is holy and acceptable before God.
  • God wants to deal with us to prepare us for what is ahead of us.
  • We need the real us, not the broken us.
  • We were all created for the glory of God.
  • We need to deal with whatever keeps us down and rise up.

Disciplines For The Disciple

Introduction
We have thus far looked at the disciplines of:

  • Prayer- secret, simple, and steadfast.
  • Meditation- contemplating God, His works, His words, and things worthy of virtue.
  • Fasting- humbling oneself before God when joined with sincere prayer.
  • Singing- which edifies the singer as well as praises God.
  • Fellowship- communing with other Christians as we engage in spiritual activities.
  • Giving- sharing sacrificially of money, time, and ability as God's grace allows.
  • Teaching- first become a student, followed by a doer and then a teacher.

Questions
  1. What discipline do you feel needs more attention today by us Christians?
  2. What discipline would you like focus on improving this season?
